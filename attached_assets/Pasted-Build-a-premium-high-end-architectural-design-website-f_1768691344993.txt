Brand Identity
- Industry: Architectural Design, Modern Homes, Smart Living
- Brand vibe: Futuristic, luxurious, minimal, confident, innovative
- Color palette: Deep navy / near-black backgrounds with cyan, teal, and electric blue neon accents

Typography

Site Structure (Hybrid)

Navigation
- Sticky header with smooth scroll links

- Responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
